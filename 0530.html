May 30, 5107 miles

A day of tourist stuff in Anchorage. Downtown is built around tourism, with a gift shop on every corner, museums, theaters, restaurants, and coffee bars all within walking distance of each other.
For Rae:

Highlights of the day included seeing yet another waterfall. This waterfall is the largest chocolate waterfall, consisting of some 3500 pounds of milk chocolate.
Merry Lou and Don also got up close and personal with one of the native bears.

We also visited Earthquake Park, a city park with interpretive signs and information on the 1964 earthquake here in Anchorage. The past 35 years have healed almost all of the wounds caused. Not many miles, just 23 today.

Wildlife for the day included: stuffed moose; stuffed deer; stuffed bears; stuffed sheep and stuffed puffins.

Tomorrow will be another sight seeing day, with little travel involved.
