June 16, 8727 miles

We started the day leaving camp at 8:00. It had rained a little late last night (fantastic lightning), but had cleared off by morning. Today is destined to be a travel day - back to the US!
Well, the day started off with good weather, but things got worse, rain, rain, and more rain. Not much in the sight seeing department today, but we did pass through some magnificent passes that just did not come out well in pictures.
Did I tell you it rained?

Towards the end of the day we had out run the rain, but it will catch back up with us soon. The views have changed a lot, a lot of rolling hills with cattle.
Since the rain was close at our heels, we checked into a hotel for the night in Deer Lodge, Montana, at 8:30 after traveling 528 miles.

Wildlife for the day included: black tail deer (1). Like I said, it was a bad day.
