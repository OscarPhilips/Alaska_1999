June 10, 6966 miles

Well, last night was so nice and cool out, little breeze, dead quiet, and all we slept in a little this AM, so we did not break camp until about 9:30AM.
Today was a travel day, without any significant sight seeing. But, there is still wildlife, such as this elk, to look at.
And the Yukon River.

Some of the beauty for the day was broken by a large tract of land that had burnt last year in a forest fire. At places you could still smell the smoke from over a year ago.

We pulled into the campgrounds, just outside Watson Lake, at 7:30 after traveling 411 miles.

Wildlife for the day included: elk (1); fox (1).

Tomorrow will be a sight seeing day, with little travel involved.
