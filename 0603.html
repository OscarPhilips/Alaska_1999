June 3, miles

Another activity day - rafting.
For my brother:

In preparation for rafting this morning, we drove portions of the river last night and watched one of the other Raft Outfitters running their maiden voyage of their Hydro Bronc. They told us it had just arrived in the mail that morning.

Kind of like a gerbil wheel for people that you walk down rivers in. Little out of control.
Our raft trip was a little more conventional -- a little. They wrap you up in a mustang suit (the kind you can jump into the Artic Ocean in and have some hopes of surviving), add a life vest, gloves, and rubber boots with thick socks, and jump in the raft.

The water was part of some glacier 10 hours ago, so it is not all that warm, 34 degrees, but the trip was fun, and we were not all that cold.

Wildlife for the day included: dall sheep (3 close, more at distance); caribou (3); and young eagles (2).

Just some incidental miles today.
